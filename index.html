My projects

These are some crochet projects ive been working on! I've also rated them on how fun they were to make if you're looking to make them yourself!

Amigurumi

A step-by-step tutorial to make amigurumi: a Japanese craft to crochet stuffed animals. This is really fun to do and you can make some awesome and original presents with this!

Tapestry Crochet

A step-by-step tutorial to make tapestry crochet: a technique to make patters on fabric, using color switches and simple crochet stitches. You can make bags, blankets, coasters, coffe holders, scarfs and pillows with this technique!
